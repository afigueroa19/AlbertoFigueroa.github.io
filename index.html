Intro

My name is Alberto Figueroa, I'm a Python enthusiast that really loves learning new ways to obtain insights from information. I started programming around 5 years ago, during my freshman year in college, but I got really involved in this world during the second year of the pandemic. At that time, I took Google's Data Analyst Certificate at coursera. This course was my first step in my journey which give me knowledge in SQL, R and Tableau. It also gave me the desire to keep on learning about Data in general.

During the last year in college, I was selected to work in a research group named GITTS. During this period, I polished my python skills and implemented multiple Machine Learning Models such as Artificial Neural Networks that were required in the state's funded research. I got my engineer degree and presented my thesis based on one of the projects I worked on the lab.

This experience help me land my first job in the tech industry at Olimpo.bet. In here I work as Data Analyst and I'm encharged of all the integrity of all Databases(SQL and NoSQL). Also, I'm encharged of all ETLs, WebScraping and all forms of BI tools required. In here I've polished even further my python knowledge. I've gained experience in Django Framework, Spark, Cloud Computing, Scraping, PowerBI, NoSQL Databases and plenty other usefull tools.

Work Experience

Olimpo.bet

Olimpo.bet is an online betting site that at the moment operates in Perú and Ecuador. Currently I work here as Data Analyst. My role in here is to enable other areas and mantain all the infrastructure behind all the BI requirements. This involves multiple Databases(SQL/NoSQL) and all forms of ETLS used.

Activities:

  • ETLS: Extract information from multiple sources, transform and load it into DataBase using PySpark.
  • Web Scraping: Extract information from multiple Web Pages, to compare against competitors. Also, to obtain a calendar of future events
  • BI tools: This includes PowerBI, automated excels, and a huge WebPage based on Django that contains KPIs for each part of the company and stakeholers.
  • Loyalty Program: Evaluate players based on specific metrics and give them points based on amount wagered.
  • SQL Queries: Some areas will required information with High Granularity for BI interpretation and they will require specific views for the info retrieval.
  • Database Management: Make sure all Databases are operative 24/7.

GITTS

Investigation Group on Advanced Telecommunication Technologies and Signal Processing (GITTS) is an investigation group with their laboratory located in Universidad Tecnológica de Panamá. I worked here from March 2022 until I accepted the offer of Olimpo.bet on January 2023. My role in here was to assist in the investigation, reading scientific papers or documentation, taking samples and programming.

Projects:

  • Desarrollo de una Plataforma de Inspección Rápida de Buques Marinos Basada en Técnicas de Sensores Remotos:

  • Desarrollo De Una Plataforma De Adquisición De Imágenes Hiperespectrales Asistida Por Técnicas Avanzadas De Aprendizaje Automatizado Para Identificación De Aptitud De Cultivos De Arroz:

Projects

Odd's Scraper

This is a small experiment of implementing Selenium and Playwright to scrape data from betting sites in Panama. This codes could be modified to write over a CSV to obtain how odds change in a time series. Also you could compare and found out which site offers better prizes.

Sport's Calendar

This project serves the purpose to generate a csv with all upcoming events using BeautifulSoup to scrape 365Scores WebPage.

Why is this useful?

  • For sport's fans:

    • Could automate the scraper to send you a mail when a game is about to start.

  • For sport related companies:

    • Could help plan their commercial/promotion plans and can be used to forecast sells.
